cPDH is Pauper Commander, but highly interactive.

cPDH is a metagame and a mindset where:

1. All legal strategies are accepted.

2. Decks are crafted to affect the game as early as possible.

3. Players agree to only make plays that help them win the game.

cPDH adheres to the Pauper Commander Rules Set & Ban List.
