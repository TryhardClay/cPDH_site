PAUPER DUEL COMMANDER TIER LIST

This is an overview of the strongest commanders for Pauper Duel Commander. PDC (also called Duel PDH) is the 1v1 version of Pauper Commander. In PDC, a player starts at 25 life and can take up to 21 commander damage from a single commander. The decks must consist of only commons with an uncommon commander. A deck must be exactly 100 cards. For more information on the rules of PDC, click HERE Currently the only card banned is Oubliette. Each commander on the list includes a sample deck list. The example decklists, while not necessarily perfect, should give you a good idea of what that archetype is able to do and what you should expect in terms of power level for each deck.

The list is broken down into 4 sections. A Tier commanders are those that consistently win against most every deck in the format and who don't have a clear counter. Tier B commanders are those that are competitive against most decks in the format and should have positive win-rates in most tournaments. Tier C commanders are those that may be competitive in certain metagames, and Tier D commanders are those that some think are viable, but need more evaluation to determine their competitiveness.

PDC is a high interaction format where early plays matter. Most of the decks are based on creature centric strategies so the most successful decks usually include a comprehensive suite of creature removal and/or counterspells. Decks which are overly reliant on their commander will struggle to compete unless they have a consistent way to protect it. Since board wipes are not very prevalent at a common rarity, control of the board is even more critical.
